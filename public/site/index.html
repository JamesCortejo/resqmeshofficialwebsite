Local Emergency Coordination

Connected With Valencia City Disaster Response

ResQMesh is designed around local emergency coordination needs in Valencia City, Bukidnon. The system supports verified residents and responders with offline messaging when floods, earthquakes, power outages, or network disruptions make normal communication unreliable.

The official City of Valencia departments directory lists the City Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office as a local department and provides cdrrmovalencia@gmail.com as its public contact email.

Engineered for Crisis Response

Our custom-engineered system bypasses cellular networks, creating a resilient local communication channel.

Zero Infrastructure

Operates entirely independently of cellular, satellite, fiber, or internet networks, relying on peer-to-peer device nodes.

LoRa Mesh Power

Uses Long Range modules to transmit text coordinates and distress signals across kilometers using dynamic multi-hop routing.

Disaster Resilient

Optimized for low power consumption, enabling devices to stay operational on small backup batteries for days during outages.
